  • ABOUT KESHI

Casey Luong (born November 4, 1994), known professionally as Keshi (stylized as keshi), is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. Recognized for his distinctive blend of lo-fi hip-hop, R&B, and indie influences, Keshi has garnered a global fanbase with his introspective lyrics and atmospheric soundscapes. Born and raised in Houston, Texas, Keshi developed an early interest in music, teaching himself to play guitar at the age of 13. Inspired by artists such as John Mayer and Ed Sheeran, he began writing songs during his teenage years, often sharing his music with friends and family. Despite his passion, Keshi initially pursued a career as a registered nurse after graduating from the University of Texas at Austin.

While working as a nurse, Keshi turned to music as a creative outlet, producing and recording songs in his bedroom studio. He uploaded these tracks to SoundCloud under the name "keshi," a childhood nickname given by his fiancée. His early releases, characterized by lo-fi beats and melancholic vocals, quickly gained traction online, earning him a loyal following and recognition within the underground music community. Keshi’s decision to leave his nursing career to pursue music full-time marked a pivotal moment in his life. His breakout EPs, including Skeletons (2019) and Band-Aids (2020), showcased his unique sound and cemented his status as a rising artist in the alternative R&B scene.

In March 2022, Keshi released his debut studio album, Gabriel, to critical acclaim, solidifying his reputation as a versatile artist and storyteller. His music often explores themes of love, heartbreak, and personal growth, delivered with emotive vocals and minimalist production. In addition to his music, Keshi has achieved significant commercial success, including sold-out tours and a dedicated online following. Known for his hands-on approach to production and songwriting, Keshi continues to push creative boundaries, influencing a new generation of artists.
